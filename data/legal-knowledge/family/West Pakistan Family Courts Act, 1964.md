## THE FAMILY COURTS ACT, 1964
(Act XXXV of 1964)
## [18 July 1964]

An Act to make provision for the establishment of Family Courts.

Preamble.– WHEREAS it  is  expedient  to  make  provision  for  the  establishment  of  Family
Courts for the expeditious settlement and disposal of disputes relating to marriage and family
affairs and for matters connected therewith;
It is hereby enacted as follows:-

- Short title, extent and commencement.– (1) This Act may be called the
