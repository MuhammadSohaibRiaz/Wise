In  exercise  of  the  powers  conferred  by  section  16  of  the  Emigration  Ordinance,  1979  (XVIII  of  1979),  the
Federal Government is pleased to make the following rules, namely:-
- Short title and commencement.
(1). These rules may be called the Emigration Rules, 1979.
(2). They shall come into force at once.
- Definitions. In these rules, unless there is anything repugnant in the subject or context:-
(a) “Association” means Association of the Overseas Employment Promoters;
(b) “bank” means any scheduled bank;
