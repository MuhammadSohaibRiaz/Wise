# WiseCase Verification Checklist

This checklist is for verifying the AI Chat History persistence and Cross-Role Synchronization updates.

## 1. AI Chat History Persistence
- [ ] **Verification Step 1**: Log in as a Client or Lawyer.
- [ ] **Verification Step 2**: Open the WiseCase Assistant (chatbot) and send at least 3-4 messages.
- [ ] **Verification Step 3**: Refresh the page entirely (F5).
- [ ] **Expected Result**: The previous conversation should be automatically reloaded and visible in the chat window.
- [ ] **Verification Step 4**: Logout and log in as a different user.
- [ ] **Expected Result**: You should NOT see the previous user's chat history (Privacy/RLS Check).

## 2. Document Analysis History Restoration
- [ ] **Verification Step 1**: Navigate to **Document Analysis** (`/client/analysis`).
- [ ] **Verification Step 2**: Upload a new document and wait for the AI analysis to complete.
- [ ] **Verification Step 3**: Go to the **History** tab.
- [ ] **Verification Step 4**: Locate the document you just uploaded and click on it.
- [ ] **Expected Result**: The UI should automatically switch to the **Analyze** tab and display the full analysis results (Summary, Risk Assessment, Recommendations) for that document.

## 3. Client Sidebar Logout Fix
- [ ] **Verification Step 1**: Log in as a Client.
- [ ] **Verification Step 2**: Click the **Logout** button in the sidebar.
- [ ] **Expected Result**: You should be redirected to the landing page, and the Supabase session should be correctly terminated (verify by trying to access `/client/dashboard` manually; it should redirect you to sign-in).

## 4. Currency Standardization ($ USD)
- [ ] **Verification Step 1**: Check the **Client Dashboard** summary cards.
- [ ] **Verification Step 2**: Check the **Payments** page.
- [ ] **Verification Step 3**: Check the **Lawyer Sidebar** (Total Earnings).
- [ ] **Verification Step 4**: Check the **Lawyer Profile** (Hourly Rate).
- [ ] **Verification Step 5**: Check the **AI Recommendations** form (Budget range).
- [ ] **Expected Result**: All instances previously showing `PKR` should now consistently show the dollar sign `$`.

## 5. Lawyer Sidebar Profile Management
- [ ] **Verification Step 1**: Log in as a Lawyer.
- [ ] **Verification Step 2**: Look at the sidebar under **Profile Management**.
- [ ] **Verification Step 3**: Click on "Professional Info", "Specializations", and "Security & Account".
- [ ] **Expected Result**: Each link should navigate you to the Profile page and automatically open the corresponding tab (Professional, Specializations, or Security).

## 6. Lawyer-Side AI Analysis Visibility
- [ ] **Verification Step 1**: Log in as a Client and analyze a document for a specific case.
- [ ] **Verification Step 2**: Log in as the Lawyer assigned to that case.
- [ ] **Verification Step 3**: Navigate to **Cases** and open the specific case.
- [ ] **Verification Step 4**: Under the **Documents** section, locate the document the client analyzed.
- [ ] **Verification Step 5**: Click the **"AI Analysis"** button next to the document.
- [ ] **Expected Result**: An "AI Analysis Results" section should appear/expand showing the summary, risk level, and recommendations generated by the AI for the lawyer to review.

## 7. Real-time Message Notifications
- [ ] **Verification Step 1**: Log in as a Client in Browser A and as a Lawyer in Browser B.
- [ ] **Verification Step 2**: From Browser A, send a message to the Lawyer.
- [ ] **Expected Result**: Browser B should instantly show a numerical badge (1) on the message icon in the header or sidebar.
- [ ] **Verification Step 3**: Click on the message icon in Browser B to go to the messages page.
- [ ] **Expected Result**: The badge should disappear once the messages are loaded/read.

## 8. Dynamic Dashboard Content (Lawyer)
- [ ] **Verification Step 1**: Log in as a Lawyer.
- [ ] **Verification Step 2**: Check **Client Testimonials**.
- [ ] **Expected Result**: The section should display actual reviews from the `reviews` table (published status) instead of the hardcoded placeholders.
- [ ] **Verification Step 3**: Check **Case Studies**.
- [ ] **Verification Step 4**: Click "Add case study", fill in details, and save.
- [ ] **Expected Result**: The new case study should appear in the list and persist after refreshing.

## 9. AI Profile Management
- [ ] **Verification Step 1**: Open AI Chat.
- [ ] **Verification Step 2**: Type "What is missing in my profile?"
- [ ] **Expected Result**: AI should list missing fields (e.g., Bio, Phone).
- [ ] **Verification Step 3**: Type "Update my bio to: 'Legal expert with 15 years experience'."
- [ ] **Expected Result**: AI should confirm the update.
- [ ] **Verification Step 4**: Check the **Profile Completion Card** on the dashboard.
- [ ] **Expected Result**: The percentage should increase and "Bio" should be marked as done instantly.

## 10. Lawyer Dashboard Tracking & Visibility
- [ ] **Verification Step 1**: Log in as a Client and book a Lawyer.
- [ ] **Verification Step 2**: Log in as the Lawyer.
- [ ] **Verification Step 3**: Observe the dashboard **Management Hub**.
- [ ] **Expected Result**: The new request appears in the **"Requests" tab** in real-time.
- [ ] **Verification Step 4**: Click "Accept" on the request.
- [ ] **Expected Result**: 
    - The request moves from "Requests" to the **"Upcoming" tab** (status: Awaiting Payment).
    - The case appears in the **"Active Cases" tab** automatically.
- [ ] **Verification Step 5**: Log in as Client and pay for the appointment.
- [ ] **Expected Result**: In the Lawyer's **"Upcoming" tab**, the appointment changes status from "Awaiting Payment" to **"Confirmed"** in real-time.

## 11. Case Completion & Review Process
- [ ] **Verification Step 1**: As a Lawyer, go to an "In Progress" Case Detail page.
- [ ] **Verification Step 2**: Select "Request Completion" from the status dropdown and Save.
- [ ] **Expected Result**: Status changes to "Completion Requested" and client is notified.
- [ ] **Verification Step 3**: Log in as the Client and open the same case.
- [ ] **Expected Result**: An action card appears with "Confirm Completion" and "Raise Dispute".
- [ ] **Verification Step 4**: Click "Raise Dispute".
- [ ] **Expected Result**: A **Dispute Modal** opens asking for a reason and description.
- [ ] **Verification Step 5**: Click "Confirm Completion".
- [ ] **Expected Result**: 
    - Case status changes to "Completed".
    - A Review Modal automatically opens.
- [ ] **Verification Step 6**: Submit a 5-star review.
- [ ] **Expected Result**: Review is saved to the database for moderation.

## 13. Communication Enhancements
- [ ] **Verification Step 1**: Log in as a Client in Browser A and as a Lawyer in Browser B.
- [ ] **Verification Step 2**: From Browser A, send a message to the Lawyer.
- [ ] **Expected Result**:

- [ ] **Verification Step 3**: Go to the Messages page in Browser B.
- [ ] **Expected Result**:

## 14. Storage & Portfolio Integration

- [ ] **Verification Step 2**: Go to the "Case Studies" section and click "Add case study".
- [ ] **Verification Step 3**: Fill in the details and upload an image thumbnail.

## 15. Conflict Resolution (Ticketing)
- [ ] **Verification Step 1**: Following Section 11, Step 4: Fill out the Dispute Modal and submit.
- [ ] **Expected Result**: Case status changes to **"Disputed"** and the modal closes.
- [ ] **Verification Step 2**: Log in as an Admin and navigate to the **"Disputes"** tab.
- [ ] **Expected Result**: The new dispute appears at the top of the list with the client's reason and description.
- [ ] **Verification Step 3**: Click **"Take Action"**, enter an Admin Note, and click **"Confirm Resolution"**.
- [ ] **Expected Result**: Dispute status changes to **"Resolved"** and the admin notes are permanently recorded.
